This website is completely fake! There is no school called Christ's Chosen. I will now describe my plan to manipulate users. I took a base template for websites and searched each of the tags I didn't know via a html help website. This allowed me to take complete control of my webpage's functions. I decided that all I needed for an authentic webpage was a stylish background, a solid format, and pictures of the fake thing I was presenting. I kept the menu style and the picture above it, but changed the photo and the spacing of the titles. I then looked for a new menu banner because the menu they had didn't fit my color scheme. I ended up photoshopping a new menu and background and putting them in the place of the previous menu and background pictures. I created the format of my webpages by implementing the table. I also looked at the websites of other schools to get a good sense of what a school's website would look like. What a fun project!
